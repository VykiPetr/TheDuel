S'r

Welcometh!

Thee might not but winneth 3 duels

in 'rd'r to winneth the tournament!

Game rules

Thee might not but chooseth from 3 positons

If 't be true thee hath chosen a base'r position yond of thy opponent

Thee striketh that gent and deflect the bloweth!

If 't be true thy position matches the opponent's

Thee might not but chooseth a new one again!

But beest careful as timeth to chooseth shall decrease!

Most wondrous of luck S'r Knight!
